The user can move the character with the arrow keys and use the space bar to shoot enemies. The user can also choose to not continue to finish the game or press start to play again. Water Temperature Check

In this project, we used the ATmega 328PB Xplained Mini to check the water temperature using a Thermistor NTC 10K Ohm and an ultrasonic sensor. The system avoids overflow when the water reaches the desired distance by using green and red LEDs to trigger a buzzer sound when the water reaches the distance.
